Q: Do you have a big purchase coming up soon?

What do credit card companies offer, besides 2 or 3% cash back? Some offers include:

  • 0% interest for 12 months as long as you make minimum monthly payments ($30-50/month)
  • Between 15,000 and 100,000 points ($150-$1,000 in free money!!) once you spend a certain amount during your first 3 months

Best practice is to earn rewards by spending money you already planned on spending (don't spend money you wouldn't otherwise spend just for rewards -- that's how they get you!!) Think about any upcoming purchases over $500.
